RADO

RADO

Luxury Swiss brand Rado have been pioneers in crafting high-end watches since 1917. So we were sure to set aside a few hours of studio time to bring their 2015 photographic campaign to life in CGI Animation.

Working with Foxtrot Papa, we created three animations for Rado’s new collection, with backgrounds tailored to showcase the bespoke materials and functionalities unique to each model, letting you take a close look at the finer details of Rado’s master craftsmanship.
