NIO

NIO

In our latest collaboration with our friends at NIO, we created a suite of CGI stills for the reveal imagery of the EC6 as part of the much anticipated NIO Day 2021.

Working on location in Barcelona with the NIO team, and shot by renowned automotive photographer Tomek Olszowski, INK carefully crafted the integration of CGI digi-car and photographic assets to showcase the luxury EV. Through the ranging dynamic CGI exteriors and intimate close ups incorporating location photography and a CGI digi-car, we reveal the all new coupé’s silhouette, panoramic moonroof, and aerodynamic design.

    These are cookies that are required for the operation of our website. They include, for example, cookies that enable you to log into secure areas of our website, use a shopping cart or make use of e-billing services.
