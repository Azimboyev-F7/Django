Polestar

Polestar

Celebrating the newest addition to Polestar's electric family, we teamed up with photographer Stefan Isaksson to launch the Polestar 4. The result is a showcase in future-focussed mobility that honours our shared values of beauty in technology.

Celebrating Polestar's fastest production car yet, our team emphasised the inherent speed and motion in Isaksson's photography. Shot in Texas at the legendary Circuit of the Americas, we reworked the track colours to evoke the trademark Polestar aesthetic. Bringing a visionary attitude to this iconic track, the visuals pay tribute to Polestar's project of innovation.

    These are cookies that are required for the operation of our website. They include, for example, cookies that enable you to log into secure areas of our website, use a shopping cart or make use of e-billing services.
