McLaren x OKX

McLaren x OKX

Stealth mode ON. The INK team worked closely with McLaren and OKX to create a CGI film and imagery that unleashed the MCL60 for its release at the Singapore 2023 and Japan 2023 Grand Prix. We luxuriously animated the car in sleek CG environments to hone in on the symbiosis of the two brands. The work confidently represents the organic fusion of McLaren's sophisticated design with bold OKX branding. Shifting between shadow and light, the determination of the MCL60 takes centre stage. This is technical mastery.
